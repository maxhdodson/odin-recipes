Overnight Oats

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup Rolled Oats
  • 1/2 cup Milk
  • 1/4 cup Greek Yogurt
  • 1 tbsp Chia Seeds
  • 1 tbsp Honey

Instructions:

  1. To start, combine rolled oats, milk, greek yogurt, chia seeds, and honey.
  2. Mix the overnight oats together until you see no clumps.
  3. Next, seal or cover with a lid, and chill in the fridge for at least 2 hours or overnight.
  4. Top with your choice of fruit or flavorings and enjoy!
